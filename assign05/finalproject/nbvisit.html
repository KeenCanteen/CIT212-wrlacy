Non-Bird Visitors

Squirrels

Size:
6"
Diet:
ALL OF MY BIRD SEED
Notes:
Almost impossible to outwit, cunning squirrels can (and will) get to every feeder and gorge themselves.

Jenkins the Squirrel

Size:
6"
Diet:
All the birdseed they want
Notes:
Jenkins is a special squirrel. They seem to have some sort of disease causing them to lose their hair. I'd feel guilty shooing Jenkins away from the feeders, so they can stay.

Coco the Cat

Size:
18"
Diet:
Birds, rodents, cat food
Notes:
We don't know who actually owns Coco, they won't let us get close enough to inspect their collar. Nonetheless, Coco is a frequent prowler around the feeders waiting for their chance to strike. The menacing feline form is somewhat diminished by the adorable clown collar.

Martha the Cat

Size:
18"
Diet:
Birds, rodents, cat food
Notes:
We do know who Martha's owners are. She doesn't come over to the bird feeder side of the house often possibly because of Coco. Very Skiddish.
